Privacy Policy

Forsyth Schools Portal
Last updated: July 11, 2026

Forsyth Schools Portal is a Chrome extension that helps Forsyth County Schools students quickly open ClassLink, Infinite Campus, Canvas, and district resources from a single dashboard.

Information we access

  • Student display name — When you visit Infinite Campus while logged in, the extension reads your visible display name from a single page element (h2.student-card__student-name) to personalize the welcome message in the extension popup.
  • ClassLink sign-in state — When you visit ClassLink, the extension checks whether a login form or app launcher is visible to show an appropriate message in the popup. It does not read usernames, passwords, or cookies.

Information we store

The extension stores the following data locally on your device using chrome.storage.local:

  • Your student display name (if detected on Infinite Campus)
  • Whether ClassLink appears to be signed in (true/false)
  • A timestamp of the last ClassLink sign-in check

Information we do NOT collect

  • Passwords, credentials, or authentication tokens
  • Grades, attendance records, assignments, or other academic records
  • Browsing history outside the specific school domains listed in the extension
  • Location data
  • Analytics, tracking, or advertising data

How data is used

Stored data is used only to personalize the extension popup (for example, showing “Welcome, [Name]”) and to display helpful sign-in prompts. Data is never sold, shared with third parties, or transmitted to external servers.

Data retention and deletion

Data remains on your device until you remove the extension or clear extension data in Chrome at chrome://extensions.

Third-party services

The extension opens official Forsyth County Schools websites (ClassLink, Infinite Campus, Canvas, and the district site) in new browser tabs when you click a dashboard tile. Those sites have their own privacy policies and login systems. This extension does not bypass or replace school authentication.

Remote code

All extension code is included in the extension package. No remote JavaScript or WebAssembly is loaded or executed.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, open an issue on the project repository: github.com/jwatson2029/forsyth-schools-portal
